About volunteering

Paperized is a place where tutors and middle or high school students from all over the world can connect and share or receive essential knowledge. This way we, as a community, could ensure that every single student would have a chance to perfectly understant and easily access the curriculum taught in schools. Our volunteers are people, who are passionate, understanding, kind and know their subjects in and out!

Could I become a volunteer?

If you would like to become a volunteer, you should fist ask yourself these questions:

  • Am I understanding and open to everyone's situation and story?
  • Do I like answering questions and helping other understant topics about my chosen subjects?
  • Do I understand my school curriculum well enough to clearly explain it to others?

If your answer to all of these questions was "yes", then you are a perfect candidate for our volunteer team! All you need to do is fill in our registration form and wait for an email from our team!
