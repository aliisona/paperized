Our Policy

No inappropriate submission of notes: Do not submit anything that demotes or demeans other users of the site. Any action of discrimination towards a persons race, religion, gender identity or ethnicity will lead to a permanent ban from the site.

Disclosure of Private Information: Do not use Paperized as a medium to discuss or present your private information. We will not be responsible for the consequences of those poor actions.

No actions of bullying or harrassment: Please refrain from using harmful & rude words. Don't bully.

Submit notes under the correct sections: When submitting your notes make sure you are submitting it under the right heading/section. For example: 'Notes about Verbs & Nouns' must be submitted under the English section and not the Physics section.

Report Abuse: If you recognize any of the rules above being broken, please do not hesitate to report if or contact us at abuse@paperized.com.
