Our Goals

  • Accessibility
    Students all over the world have trouble accessing learning resources due to monetary limitations or restrictions. According to NBC, tutoring can cost up to tens of thousands USD a year. The tutoring market is expected to rise to 200K million by 2026 in the US, only making learning more expensive.

  • Online
    Existing resources are messy and students spend more time filtering and searching through than actually reading an easy-to-use site.

  • Inclusiveness
    Sites that do exist do not compensate for students who have trouble reading in certain colors and presented fonts.
